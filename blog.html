Smile Dental Care

Services

Cavity Protection

Cavity protection involves regular brushing, flossing, and using fluoride to prevent tooth decay and maintain oral health.

Teeth Whitening

Teeth whitening brightens your smile by removing stains and discoloration, offering a radiant, healthy appearance with professional treatments or at-home solutions.

Dental Feelings

Dental fillings restore damaged or decayed teeth by filling cavities with materials like amalgam, composite, or porcelain, improving function and appearance.

Implant Dentistry

Implant dentistry involves placing titanium implants into the jawbone to replace missing teeth, offering a durable, natural-looking solution for improved oral function and aesthetics.

Dental Vineers

Dental veneers are thin, custom-made shells placed over the front surface of teeth to improve appearance, correcting issues like discoloration, chips, or misalignment

General Checkup and Teeth Cleaning

A general checkup and teeth cleaning maintain oral health by detecting issues early and removing plaque and tartar buildup

Root Conal

A root canal is a dental procedure to treat infection or damage in the tooth's pulp, relieving pain and saving the tooth.

Crowns,Bridges and Dentures

Crowns restore damaged teeth, bridges replace missing ones, and dentures offer removable replacements for lost teeth, enhancing function and appearance.

Braces

Braces are dental devices used to straighten teeth, correct bite issues, and improve overall oral alignment for a healthier, more confident smile.

Tooth Extractions

Tooth extractions involve removing a damaged or decayed tooth to prevent further oral health issues, often performed when a tooth cannot be saved.

Wismdom Teeth Removal

Wisdom teeth removal is a surgical procedure to extract the third molars, often due to impaction, crowding, or infection, ensuring better oral health.
